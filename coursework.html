Coursework

While at Bellevue College, I've taken a couple of different courses. My favorite so far though, has been ART 245: Watercolor 1, taught by Victor Sandblom.

I'm only half way through the class since I am taking it this quarter (Winter 2019), but so far I'm loving the class.

The assignments so far have gotten me to try new things like combining a sheep with a tiger and painting different cryptids.

Before taking this course, I had a fairly simple understanding of watercolor and how it works. But even in just five weeks, this class has taugh me different techniques on how to apply the watercolor through washes or to create cool, unique patterns by letting the pigment drop onto wet paper.

I really recommend this course to anyone who enjoys letting go and painting away.
